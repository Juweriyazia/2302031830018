About Us

Music connects people in ways nothing else can. We wanted to create a space where collaboration goes beyond just sharing songs—where you can discover new music, build playlists together in real time, and create lasting memories through sounds. We believe music is best when shared. Our platform was built for music lovers who want to create, curate, and collaborate on playlists with friends, family, and the community. Whether you're crafting the perfect road trip mix, a party anthem lineup, or a chill study session playlist, we make it easy to bring everyone's favorite tracks together in one place—through sound.
